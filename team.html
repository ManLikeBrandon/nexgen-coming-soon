The People

Driven by People
Who Believe in People

Our team brings together expertise in education, community development, communications, and social finance — all united by a genuine belief that what we're building matters.

Leadership Team

The People Who Drive the Work

SM

Dr. Sarah Mokoena

Executive Director

Twenty years in education and social development. A doctorate in Educational Leadership, and a track record of building programs that actually hold up. She founded NexGen Leaders because she was tired of watching potential go to waste.

JO

James Okonkwo

Director of Programs

James went through the Leadership Academy himself — Cohort 2. He now oversees all four programs, which means he knows exactly what works and what doesn't. He holds a master's in Youth Development and uses it daily.

LD

Lerato Dlamini

Head of Communications

Lerato spent a decade in journalism before moving into brand strategy. She joined NexGen to tell a story she believed in. Her job is to make sure the work speaks — clearly, honestly, and to the right people.

TN

Thabo Nkosi

Community Engagement Manager

Thabo built his career in community organising before he joined us. He manages relationships with schools, civic organisations, and local partners — making sure our programs reach the young people who need them most, not just those who are easiest to reach.

AR

Amara Reyes

Grants & Finance Manager

Amara comes from impact investing. She manages the Youth Impact Grants program and oversees the foundation's finances with the same question in mind: how do we make every rand count? So far, the answer has been disciplined, transparent, and auditable.

KP

Kwame Patel

Mentorship Network Coordinator

Kwame recruits, trains, and supports every mentor in the network. He takes the matching process seriously — because a bad match is worse than no match. He's also the person mentors call when a session gets hard. He picks up.

Governance

Board of Directors

Our board provides strategic oversight and accountability — ensuring the foundation operates with integrity and stays true to its mission.

CN

Prof. Claire Naidoo

Board Chair

Academic and higher education strategist. Thirty years developing leaders at university level. She chairs the board because she believes in the work, not because it's prestigious.

DM

David Mensah

Deputy Chair

Social entrepreneur and co-founder of NexGen Leaders. He's been in the room since the beginning and holds the organisation to the founding vision — respectfully and persistently.

FO

Fatima Osei

Treasurer

Chartered accountant specialising in nonprofit finance. Her job is to make sure the numbers are honest. They are.

RK

Ravi Kapoor

Board Member

Senior private sector executive with a long record of corporate social investment. He asks the hard questions about sustainability that the team occasionally doesn't want to answer — and he's always right to ask.

ZA

Zanele Adams

Board Member

Nonprofit law specialist. Keeps the organisation compliant, protected, and governed properly. Not glamorous. Absolutely essential.

MT

Marcus Trent

Alumni Representative

Leadership Academy Cohort 1, now a community leader and entrepreneur. Marcus is on the board so that the people who actually went through our programs have a voice in how they're run.
