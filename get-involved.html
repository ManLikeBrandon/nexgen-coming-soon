Program Applications

Apply to NexGen

Tell us about yourself and what draws you to our programs. We read every application and respond to all of them — whether it's a yes, a not yet, or a suggestion for a better fit.

We aim to respond within three weeks of each intake closing date.

📝

Step 1 — Submit Your Application

Complete the form. All fields are required. Be honest — that's what we're looking for.

📞

Step 2 — Short Interview

Shortlisted applicants get a 20-minute conversation with someone from our team.

🎉

Step 3 — Welcome to NexGen

Successful applicants receive an offer, onboarding details, and a welcome pack that doesn't feel like a corporate handbook.

Mentorship Network

Become a Mentor

Our mentors are professionals who built careers worth learning from — and who are willing to share that honestly with someone earlier in the journey.

The commitment is six months, at least one structured meeting per month. We provide a conversation framework, training, and ongoing support. You won't be left to figure it out alone.

"Mentoring changed my perspective as much as it changed his."
— Mentor, Cohort 3
