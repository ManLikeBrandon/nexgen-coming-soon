Your Impact

What Your
Gift Does

Over 85% of every rand donated goes directly to programs, scholarships, and participant support. Here's exactly what different amounts do:

R250

Workshop Materials

Workbooks, resources, and materials for one participant to attend a full-day skills workshop.

R500

One Month of Mentorship

Covers the coordination, training, and administration behind one month of structured mentorship for a participant.

R1 000

Scholarship Contribution

One month toward a participant's Leadership Academy fees — for someone who can't otherwise afford to be there.

R5 000

Youth Impact Grant

Puts seed money into a young person's hands to run a real community project, with six months of support alongside it.

R10 000+

Full Program Sponsorship

Sponsors one person's entire 12-month Leadership Academy journey — from application to graduation.

100% Accountability

We publish an annual impact report with full financial disclosure. Admin overhead stays under 15%. The rest goes to programs.
