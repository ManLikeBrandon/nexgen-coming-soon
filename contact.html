Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Who can apply to the Leadership Academy? +

The Leadership Academy is open to people between 16 and 25. We actively work to make our cohorts diverse — we're not interested in picking the easiest people to develop. Financial need does not disqualify you. We have full and partial scholarships available; just indicate your situation on the application and we'll follow up.

When do applications open? +

We run two intakes per year — January and July. Applications typically open 8 weeks before each start date. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to be notified.

Is there a cost to participate? +

Some programs carry a nominal participation fee. It's there because commitment matters — not to exclude anyone. No young person is turned away for financial reasons. Full and partial scholarships are available, and we handle those conversations with care.

How are donations used? +

Over 85% of every rand donated goes directly to programs, scholarships, and participant support. We publish an annual impact report with full financial disclosure. Our administrative overhead is deliberately kept under 15%.

Can my organisation partner with NexGen Leaders? +

Yes. Partnership packages range from program sponsorship and internship placements to event co-branding and employee engagement programs. Start the conversation at partnerships@nexgenleaders.org — we'll take it from there.
