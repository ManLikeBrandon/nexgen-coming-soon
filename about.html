Who We Are

Our Story, Mission
& Vision

NexGen Leaders started with one observation: a lot of young people are ready to lead, but the path forward isn't clear. Not because they lack ambition — they have plenty of that.

How It Started

Built on a Simple Observation

Our founders spent years working with young people across education, community development, and business. The same thing kept showing up: capable, driven people who hit walls — not because they weren't good enough, but because no one had given them the structure, the connections, or the honest feedback they needed to grow.

NexGen Leaders was built to change that. Not with motivational events or short courses that feel good in the moment, but with programs that go deep and stick — cohorts, mentors, real projects, and a community that holds people accountable over time.

We've grown from a pilot cohort of 30 to a network of 500+ alumni across 15 communities. The mission hasn't changed. The scale has.

See Our Programs
"The world is shaped by those who show up."

We exist to make sure the next generation shows up — prepared, connected, and clear about what they're building.

Foundation Timeline
2019 Foundation established. First cohort of 30 young people enrolled in the Leadership Academy pilot.
2020 Mentorship Network launched. Programs moved online during the pandemic — no cohort was cancelled.
2022 Youth Impact Grants launched. Over R80 000 disbursed to 14 youth-led community projects in year one.
2024 Expanded to 15 communities. 500+ alumni. First Annual NexGen Leadership Summit draws 300+ people.
Why We Exist

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To develop young leaders who are skilled, self-aware, and genuinely ready to build something.

Through real programs, honest mentorship, access to funding, and a community that stays active long after formal programs end.

Our Vision

Communities shaped by young leaders who know what they stand for and have the tools to act on it.

A generation that leads not for status, but because they understand what's at stake — and they're ready for the responsibility.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🎯

Purpose

We ask the hard questions: what are you building, and why? Leaders without a clear answer make noise. We help people find the signal.

🔍

Integrity

We say what we mean, do what we say, and expect the same from participants — in programs, in feedback, and in how they carry the NexGen name.

🌍

Equity

Talent is everywhere. Opportunity isn't. Our programs are designed specifically to reach young people who wouldn't otherwise get a seat at the table.

🤝

Community

Alumni hire each other. They co-found things. They show up for each other years after their programs end. That's not accidental — we build for it.

📈

Excellence

We set high bars and we mean them. Not to filter people out — to signal that this is serious, and that we'll take it as seriously as you do.

🔄

Honest Improvement

We've changed our programs based on what wasn't working, and we'll keep doing that. We track what actually changes in people's lives, not what looks good in a report.
