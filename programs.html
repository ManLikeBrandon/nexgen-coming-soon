What We Do

Four Pathways.
One Direction.

Each program is built around what young leaders actually need at a specific stage — structured enough to deliver real outcomes, flexible enough to meet people where they are.

How We Work

Development That Goes Deeper

Too many leadership programs are built around inspiration — good speakers, a certificate, a day that felt energising and changed nothing. We're not interested in that.

Every NexGen program has clear outcomes, measurable milestones, and real expectations. Participants leave with actual work in their portfolios, actual relationships in their corner, and a clearer picture of what they're capable of.

📋

Real Curriculum

Evidence-informed frameworks — applied to real situations, not textbook exercises.

👥

Cohort Learning

You grow alongside the same group the whole way through. Accountability, trust, honest feedback.

📊

We Track What Matters

Not just completion rates. We follow up with alumni to understand what actually changed.

🔗

Alumni Network

Graduation is the beginning. Over 500 alumni stay connected — many stay in touch with their cohort for years.

Flagship Program

Leadership Academy

Twelve months. One cohort. Real leadership experience you can point to. The Academy combines structured curriculum with group sessions, individual coaching, and a capstone project that each participant designs and runs themselves.

You won't just learn frameworks — you'll practise them, struggle with them, and come out the other side with a portfolio of work and a peer group that holds you to account.

Duration 12 Months
Age Range 16–25 Years
Format Hybrid
Cohort Size 25–30 People
Apply Now
🔗
Mentorship

Mentorship Network

Six months. One mentor. Real conversations about the things that textbooks skip. We pair aspiring young leaders with professionals who have built careers across business, government, education, and the social sector.

We provide structure so sessions don't become vague. Mentors commit to at least one session per month — and almost all of them extend beyond the program. Both sides consistently say it gives back as much as it asks.

Duration 6 Months
Format 1-on-1
Sessions Monthly (min.)
Open To All Ages
Apply as Mentee Become a Mentor

Grants & Funding

Youth Impact Grants

If you have a bold idea for your community and the drive to see it through, we want to fund it. Grants from R500 to R5 000, with six months of implementation support alongside the money.

We don't just write cheques and leave. Grant recipients get financial coaching, project management guidance, and a check-in structure that helps them actually deliver — not just start.

Grant Range R500–R5 000
Support Period 6 Months
Open To Under 30
Cycles Twice Yearly

Events & Workshops

Workshops & Summits

Open to anyone — not just current program participants. Monthly workshops cover things people actually need: public speaking, financial literacy, conflict navigation, how to run a meeting that isn't a waste of time.

Our Annual Leadership Summit brings together hundreds of young leaders, mentors, and partners for a full day of sessions, keynotes, and the kind of conversations that tend to change things.

Annual Summit Once a Year
